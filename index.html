About Greg

I'm a Master's Student in the Advanced Reactor and Fuel Cycle group at the Department of Nuclear, Plasma, and Radiological Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research focuses on pyroprocessing diversion scenarios in the Cyclus fuel cycle simulator. As a part of my research, I am developing Pyre, a pyroprocessing module for Cyclus to simulate a variety of pyroprocessing plant configurations. My interests include non-proliferation, fuel reprocessing and software development.
